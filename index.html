Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagne alla Bolognese is an institution, typical for Sunday lunches.

This rich and tasty dish originates from the Emilia region, specifically, from the city of Bologna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ragu': Recipe here
  • Besciamella sauce: Recipe here
  • Parmesan and butter

Steps

  1. Lasagne sheets can be used without precooking only if they are fresh. If you have the dry ones cook them in boiling water according to the instructions of the package (usually4-5 minutes).
  2. Spread three spoon of ragù into the bottom of a earthenware ovenproof dish (25x35cm approx.). Follow with a layer of lasagne sheets, add a third of your ragù and a third of béchamel. Repeat the process (sheets, ragù and béchamel) two more time and top with grated parmesan.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Bake the lasagne for 20 minutes until beginning to brown on top. Leave the dish 20 minutes before serving.
  4. Buon appetito!
