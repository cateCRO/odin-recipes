Ingredients for a lasagna serving 4

  • 500 ml of milk
  • 50 gr of butter
  • 50 gr of flour

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add flour and stir until the mixture becomes like a cream.
  3. Pour in very warm milk and whisk vigorously until you have a smooth white sauce.
  4. Bring the sauce to a boil, reduce heat and cook for at least 10 minutes.
